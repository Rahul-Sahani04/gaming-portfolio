Every high-stakes game moment demands complete focus. Sekiro boss fights, Spider-Man platinum runs, CoD competitive — all require a level of present-moment execution that filters out everything except the task at hand.

This is the branch that separates knowing from doing. You can understand a problem perfectly and still fail to execute — because execution requires sustained attention, not just insight.

Focus and execution are the delivery mechanism for all other skills.
