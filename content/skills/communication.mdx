Gaming taught me that poor callouts cost rounds. When a teammate says "he's over there," the round is lost. When they say "B site, corner left, AK," you win.

Precision matters. In code reviews, in Slack messages, in standups — vague communication multiplies misunderstanding. Clear communication compounds alignment.

The other half of communication is listening. The best teammates aren't the loudest — they're the most present. This habit of active listening has made me a better engineer and collaborator.
