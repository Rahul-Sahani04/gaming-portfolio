Solo play teaches you to rely on yourself. Multiplayer teaches you something harder: how to make yourself useful to others.

Every cooperative game mode — from Call of Duty squad operations to Minecraft collaborative builds — has shaped how I think about team dynamics, communication overhead, and the subtle art of making teammates feel capable.

Social skills aren't soft skills. They're system skills. Human systems are just more unpredictable than code — and that makes them more interesting to learn.
