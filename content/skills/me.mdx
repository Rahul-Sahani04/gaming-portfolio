At the center of every skill tree is the player. Not the character, not the build — the person behind the controller.

Gaming taught me that no two players approach the same challenge the same way. The skills I've built aren't borrowed from a template — they emerged from thousands of hours of deliberate play, failure, and reflection.

This node represents the convergence of every lesson learned, every boss defeated, every team coordinated. It's not a static identity but an evolving system.
