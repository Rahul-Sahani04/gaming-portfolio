Elden Ring killed me hundreds of times. Each death felt like a door slamming shut. But each one also left a mark: a new piece of information, a pattern recognized, a route not to take again.

Resilience as a skill is the compounding effect of all those closed doors becoming understood. Eventually, you stop seeing them as failures and start seeing them as the research process.

This mindset transferred directly to engineering. Build, break, understand, rebuild — faster every cycle.
