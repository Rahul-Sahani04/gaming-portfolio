Sekiro combat is unforgiving of emotional reactions. Panic-rolling into a boss's attack is a reflex born from anxiety, not assessment. Mastery requires emotional regulation as much as technical skill.

In Call of Duty, going on tilt after a bad round cascades — bad decisions compound into losing streaks. Learning to reset the mental state between engagements was as valuable as learning map positions.

Self-control under pressure is the skill that protects all your other skills from being overridden by stress. It's the guard rail on your decision-making system.
