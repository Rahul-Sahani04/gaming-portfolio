There's no prescribed way to build in Minecraft. You're given raw materials and an empty world. What emerges is a direct expression of creative thought.

Spider-Man's traversal system embodies creative flow — the physics engine rewards creative routing, finding unexpected shortcuts through sheer experimentation.

Creativity in engineering means proposing a solution nobody asked for because it's genuinely better. It's the refusal to accept "because that's how it's done" as an answer.
