Cognitive skills form the bedrock of everything else. Before you can collaborate, endure, or focus — you must be able to *think clearly*.

From optimizing resource chains in Minecraft to decoding Elden Ring's intricate lore and enemy tells, games have been a constant cognitive workout.

These skills compound. Strong critical thinking sharpens strategic planning. Better spatial awareness deepens problem-solving. The tree grows outward from this root.
