Sekiro strips away every crutch. You can't grind your way past a boss — you have to *understand* it. Every attack pattern is a puzzle requiring analysis, not reaction.

This kind of critical thinking carries directly into engineering: reviewing code for hidden assumptions, questioning requirements before building, and evaluating trade-offs before committing to an architecture.

The discipline of pausing to assess before acting — that's a skill games trained into me.
