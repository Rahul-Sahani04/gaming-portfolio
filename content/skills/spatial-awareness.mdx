Spider-Man's traversal teaches spatial awareness at a visceral level — reading building heights, web-point angles, and swing arcs through Manhattan becomes instinctive.

Elden Ring's world design layers dungeons, cliffs, and hidden passages in ways that reward players with strong spatial models. The map isn't just a guide — it's a mental architecture exercise.

This skill directly fuels my work with Three.js, CSS layouts, and system architecture. The ability to think in three dimensions — structure, depth, relationship — shapes how I design both interfaces and infrastructure.
