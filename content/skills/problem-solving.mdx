Elden Ring doesn't hand you solutions. It presents a hostile world and trusts you to figure it out — through observation, iteration, and creative experimentation.

That same instinct drives how I approach technical challenges. See the problem clearly. Isolate variables. Hypothesis. Test. Iterate.

Minecraft reinforced this through a different lens: constraint-driven creativity. With limited materials, you're forced to invent elegant solutions rather than brute-force your way through.
