Sekiro doesn't let you level up or grind away difficulty. Every time you die — and you will die — it's feedback. The game is saying: *look closer*.

That reframe changed how I approach failure in real life. A failed deployment isn't a setback — it's a diagnostic. A rejected PR isn't criticism — it's calibration.

Resilience is the meta-skill. It's what allows you to keep engaging with difficult things long enough to become good at them.
