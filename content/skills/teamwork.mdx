Call of Duty squad modes are a masterclass in real-time teamwork under pressure. Role clarity, callout discipline, covering flanks — it all maps directly to engineering team dynamics.

Coordinating large Minecraft builds with friends taught me how to delegate, how to align on a vision, and how to contribute to someone else's creative direction without ego.

Teamwork is about making yourself a reliable node in a network, not just a high performer in isolation.
