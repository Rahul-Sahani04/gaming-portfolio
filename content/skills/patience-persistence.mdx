Beating Malenia in Elden Ring required hundreds of attempts across multiple sessions. There were nights where quitting felt rational. But grit isn't stubbornness — it's the deliberate choice to stay invested in long-term progress over short-term comfort.

Minecraft's largest builds take weeks. The reward isn't just the finished structure — it's the discipline that sustained the effort.

Grit in software looks like: reading documentation you don't understand yet, debugging an issue for three hours without escalating prematurely, and shipping things that aren't perfect but are *done*.
