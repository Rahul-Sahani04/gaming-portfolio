## About the Project

Welcome to **Indian Heritage - SIH (VSTARS)**, a project that aims to showcase the rich cultural heritage of India through an interactive and immersive website. As part of the Smart India Hackathon 2023, our team has developed this platform to celebrate the diverse history, art, and architecture of India and promote tourism and cultural awareness.

## Features

- **Interactive Map**: Explore the various regions of India and discover iconic landmarks, historical sites, and cultural attractions.
- **Virtual Tours**: Embark on virtual tours of famous monuments, temples, and heritage sites to experience the beauty and grandeur of Indian architecture.
- **Educational Content**: Learn about the history, significance, and cultural importance of different heritage sites through engaging multimedia content.
- **User Engagement**: Participate in quizzes, challenges, and interactive activities to test your knowledge and learn more about Indian heritage.
- **Community Platform**: Connect with other users, share your experiences, and contribute to the preservation and promotion of Indian heritage.

## Technologies Used

- **Frontend**: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Tailwind CSS for responsive and interactive web design.
- **Backend**: Node.js, Express, MongoDB for server-side logic, API integration, and database management.
